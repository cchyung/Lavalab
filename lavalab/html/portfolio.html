SimplySample

Why do 95% of women refuse to buy makeup online? Well, for many reasons, most notably, not being able to try-on the product first. Simply Sample seeks to resolve these issues by enabling a consumer to try on makeup products before they buy the full version - all from the comfort of your own home!

Mundo

Mundo is a revolutionary app that changes the way we interact transculturally. It combines a messaging platform with translation services so you can communicate in the language that you choose!

Vibe

VIBE spices up your day, bringing you closer to local events happening around you with social discovery. Using geolocation services, crowd sourced videos, and live feeds, you'll get to see the vibe of any event, whether it's a party or a tailgate, before you decide to go.

LUMOS

Lumos is a safety wearable that instantly connects students to emergency services. With the touch of a button, it transmits location and discretely sends an alert to get help in times of need.

Waitless

Waitless is a mobile app to optimize your night. We crowdsource wait time information for restaurants and bars, combining travel time to give you a complete metric of how long it will take to actually be seated. Is the wait for your favorite restaurant too long? The discovery feature gives you similar options in the area with a shorter wait. Contribute to the Waitless community by rating wait times yourself, and earn coupons to use at the restaurants you visit.

Tommy Bot

Tommy Bot is a chatbot that makes the lives of USC students easier by collecting all of USC's information in an easily accessible and friendly interface. Just message him on Facebook! Find his home at facebook.com/usctommybot/.

Bridge

Contrary to most of the dating apps on the market, Bridge matches individuals without revealing each other's physical appearances and building long lasting friendships and relationships.

Zap

Zap is the new way to challenge your friends. Simply Zap a friend to an exciting challenge by setting parameters and a time limit. Now, it's up to the challenger to complete the challenge with picture or video proof. When not challenging or doing challenges, you can scroll through feeds of challenges being done by your friends or in your area.

Jam Jar

Jam Jar is a collaborative music sharing application that also allows users to search through multiple music streaming platforms in a new, seamless layout.

Cache

Cache is an iOS application that allows users to painlessly save for several weeks for the purpose of purchasing a desired high-end item.

Voya

On Voya, you get to explore and discover locations while simultaneously building a bucket list of travel wishes that you are able to compare and share with friends.

Humanyze

Congrats to Humanyze as Spring 2016's Demo Night winner!
Humanyze creates automatic user interface experiments to determine the optimal user experience. Our core software is a free web framework that creates UX tests then measures how different designs affect statistics like button taps, user engagement times, and conversion rates. Using the information we collect, we determine behavioral trends and offer metric driven design consulting.

Utique

At Utique, we're addressing the pain points of online shopping. Oftentimes, we have a hard time quickly finding clothes that match our style while browsing through the large collection of online shopping stores. Utique is working to build a curated boutique that suggests clothes similar to your style based on your personality. We are building a database of clothes from all popular shopping destinations that match your needs perfectly!

Docket

Docket is an application that curates all of your activity, event, and experience needs in real time based on location and user reviews. The app allows users to explore the local environment by connecting individuals with small businesses and through the gamut to large scale event organizers over a ProductHunt-come-Yik Yakesque interface that will allow you to live your life to the fullest.

Augment Analytics

Augment Analytics is a company that focuses on data capture and its use to provide marketing solutions for mass scale optimization. Using RIFD technology, Augment Analytics tracks and manages data using Bluetooth relay. Our goal is to help provide clients with clear straight forward data about their customers. We cater to music festivals, conventions, casinos and hotels.

Hexcare

Wearables are great but how much actionable sense does the data really mean to you? Hexcare hopes to aggregate your personal wearable data and map it across the board with those within your demographic to show just really where you stand.

Dapper

Imagine receiving instant feedback with your closest group of friends moments before you make the decision to buy or wear on your night out. Dapper curates excellent fashion by starting at the ground level, your friends.

Chek

Loop

Vennue

Parents are out of town and you want to throw a party but last time you took that risk, Mom’s porcelain vase broke. Vennue allows you to rent out event space on a budget. Think AirBnB but for events and with one major twist, reverse auctioning.

SphereTECH

Exchanging and effectively recording people's contact details is ad hoc and difficult. This is not surprising considering the surplus of ways of exchanging details; from the traditional business card, to the modern phone 'bump' to the random napkin with contact information scribbled on it, our connections get scattered and lost. SphereTECH changes all of this by allowing us to exchange our contact information in a way that is familiar to all of us, through a handshake.

Steemy

This hands-free steaming device was conceived and prototyped by a team that felt there was no college and portable alternative to ironing and steaming closets. This product would fit delicately in a closet space and be easily stored for later use. By using an enclosed bag with thick steam, this product would quickly smooth out wrinkles within minutes.

Kit

Kit is a web platform that helps beauty enthusiasts discover new products, find inspiration, and become part of a global community.
